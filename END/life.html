Saint's Assembly of God


Welcome to the official website of the Saints' Assembly of God in South Africa. We aim to spread the gospel across many nations and make the name of Jesus Christ known in all its beauty and truth. With prayer, praise, worship to the Lord through His ministers - the apostles, prophets, evangelists, pastors and teachers - every convocation day, we believe that the Word does not return to God without having accomplished God's will for us (Isaiah 55:11). If the Lord has been good to you, why not testify His name? Join us!
