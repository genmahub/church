Saints’ Assembly of God

Our Activities

We have Sunday, Tuesday, and Friday services: gathering, praying, singing with joy, and listening to the message sent by God through His ministers.

Youth meetings are held where the youth leader teaches and answers questions that youth can’t directly ask their parents.

Youth outings provide a day of enjoyment and an opportunity to practice what they’ve learned by sharing with others.

We also have fasting days, mostly on Sunday, where the entire church fasts seeking a deep connection with God.

Father/Mother meetings are held where they meet with the servants of God and share questions or ideas.
